Hey, I'm Marco Pennekamp

I am a software engineer with a strong interest in functional programming and programming languages. My current go-to language is Scala, but I have worked and do work with an extensive range of languages. These include Haskell, Javascript, Python, Java, Lua, Typescript, and C++.

I love languages, both artificial and natural. I am interested in the design and implementation of programming languages, and have created smaller languages such as bryg and eemo, as well as concepts for larger languages, of which the most recent one is lore.

My native language is German, but I am equally comfortable with English, in which I do most of my reading and writing. I still come across new words that I want to learn and use in my own writing. Simply looking up a word doesn't cut it. This is why I am creating Diceres. Diceres combines a dictionary with a spaced-repetition system, so you can learn your words directly from the dictionary, without having to write your own flashcards or learn from predetermined lists.
